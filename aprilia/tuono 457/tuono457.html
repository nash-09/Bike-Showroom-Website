Aprilia Tuono 457 Summary

Aprilia Tuono 457 is a sports bike available in only 1 variant and 1 colour. The Aprilia Tuono 457 is powered by 457cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 46.9 bhp and a torque of 43.5 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Aprilia Tuono 457 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Tuono 457 bike weighs 175 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12.7 liters.

The Aprilia Tuono 457 is the latest twin-cylinder naked streetfighter from the Italian brand in India. The motorcycle is based on the RS 457 and, like the fully-faired bike, it is manufactured in India and exported globally.

Unlike other Tuono models that get a semi-fairing inspired by the equivalent RS model, the Tuono 457 features a unique bikini fairing and tank extensions to set it apart from the RS 457. The rest of the design, from the fuel tank to its tail section, is identical to the fully-faired sibling. Overall, the styling looks sporty and aggressive.

The same 457cc, parallel-twin engine from the RS 457 powers the Aprilia Tuono 457. The liquid-cooled motor produces 47.6bhp and a peak torque of 43.5Nm. The engine is paired with a slick-shifting six-speed gearbox that benefits from a slipper clutch.

The features list of the Aprilia Tuono 457 comprises full LED lighting, five-inch colour TFT display with Bluetooth connectivity, multiple ride modes, traction control system, and dual-channel ABS. The TFT console also allows the rider to receive calls and control music on the go.

Under the bodywork, there's a twin-spar aluminium frame suspended on preload-adjustable USD front forks and a monoshock at the rear. Meanwhile, braking duties are handled by a 320mm front disc and a 220mm rear disc. The motorcycle rides on 17-inch wheels wrapped in TVS Protorq tyres.

The Aprilia Tuono 457 is a direct rival to the Yamaha MT-03 but faces competition from the KTM 390 Duke and the Royal Enfield Guerrilla 450.
